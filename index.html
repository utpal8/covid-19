Prevention from Corona Virus

stay home, stay safe

how to prevent

how to prevent virus

Wash your place

Disinfect frequently used items and touched surfaces often.

Maintain distance

Avoid places that are crowded and maintain physical distancing of at least 6 feet.

Don't touch face

Kindly avoid touching your face, especially your eyes, nose and mouth.

Wash your hand

Practise good hygiene: cover coughs and sneezes, don't touch your face,and wash hands often at least 20 seconds.

Use napkin

Cough or sneeze into a tissue or your elbow. Throw the tissue away and wash your hands after.

Wear a mask

It helps to reduce the risk of spread both by close contact and by airborne transmission.

take precautions against covid-19

things you should DO

wash your hand

Wash your hands often with soap and water. Use hand sanitizer if soap and water aren’t available.

always wear a mask

Wear a mask that covers your nose and mouth to help protect yourself and others.

stay home during fever

Most people with COVID-19 have mild illness and can recover at home without medical care. Do not leave your home, except to get medical care. Do not visit public areas.

things you should NOT DO

avoid close contact with animals

People with suspected or confirmed COVID-19 should avoid contact with animals, including pets, livestock, and wildlife.

avoid close contact with peoples

maintain 6 feet between the person who is sick and other household members.

avoid crowded area

the virus that causes COVID-19 can transmit more easily between people when there are many in close proximity.
